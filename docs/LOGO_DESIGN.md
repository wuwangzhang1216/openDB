# OpenDB Logo & Brand Guide

## Logo

The OpenDB logo is a stylized **"D"** inside a rounded square, with a horizontal bar that extends beyond the left edge вҖ” representing "open."

- **Outer shape**: Rounded square (`rx="17"` on 80Г—80 viewBox)
- **Inner mark**: A "D" formed by a quadratic BГ©zier path
- **Slit**: A horizontal rectangle at vertical center, extending past the left edge to `x=0`
- **Concept**: The slit "opens" the container вҖ” Open + DB

### Sizes

The icon is designed to work from 16px to any size. Minimum clear space = height of the slit.

## Misuse

Do not:
- Rotate or skew the logo
- Recolor the inner mark
- Use low opacity or as watermark
- Stretch or distort proportions
- Add shadows or effects
- Add outlines or borders
